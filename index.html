Controls

Arrow keys: move cursor / switch tabs
F: "A button" - select / choose
D: "B button" - back
S: more info (units, skills, items)
E: end turn
V: skip text / toggle 3x speed

Supported browsers: Chrome, Firefox. Mobile devices are not supported.


Release notes for demo-v0.01

  • There's a start menu! And a story!
  • The four player units each have their basic skill. (Arrow has a healing skill.)
  • Use V to skip text, or to play the game at 3x speed.
  • Weapon stats are now displayed when choosing your weapon. You can also view weapon stats by pressing S while on the "Items" tab of the UnitInfoWindow.
  • Press F while the cursor is over an enemy to view that enemy's movement and attack range.
  • This version's Hard difficulty is the difficulty from earlier demos.
  • A manga adaptation of Aqua Emblem will begin serialization in the August issue of Jump Square!
