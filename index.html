Release notes for demo-v0.0001

  • Luciana and Kenji join the fray. Now all of the members of Ace's mercenary group, "Hellhounds", have assembled!
  • If any of your units die, you lose!! Be careful. You can't yet use Arrow's First Aid skill, so Ace, Arrow, and Kenji have all received a temporary +10 buff to HP.
  • The weapon triangle is here. Swords beat axes, axes beat lances, and lances beat swords.
  • Use terrain to your advantage. Standing on a forest gives you big defensive bonuses!
  • Press E to end your turn early.
  • Some enemies won't move, while others will move to attack you if you enter their range.
This version has been tested in Firefox and Chrome. Please tell me if you find a bug.
